The College of Computing and Information Sciences is the College division comprised of Computer Science, Information Technology and Information management
Founded on SY 2022 - 2023 it is a relatively new college that has a current population of 180 student, regular and socialized combined
The dean of this College is Dr. Cesar Alipiz Tecson PhD. graduated with honors in Ateneo de Davao University and currently owning large tech company

 

 

 

Our Mission

  The mission of the College of Computing and Information Sciences is to be able to teach adn nurture people who want to learna nd grasp the future of technological evolution and AI. We always stay up to date with the current discoveries and innovations of our field and aim to constantly give our students the latest information. We create the bridge for the future of our students and create a better environment that nurures friendliness and solidarity amongst its students so that it may nutures ones passion for learning. The power is in the hands of the students and we shall be the one to reveal those powers hidden from within.
